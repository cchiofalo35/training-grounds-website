Our Team

Meet the Coaches

Our dedicated coaching team, led by Head Coach Scott, brings a wealth of experience, passion, and heart to everything we do. Their commitment to skill, creativity, and connection is what makes Training Grounds more than just a place to train.

Head Coach & Founder

Scott Ireland

BJJ, Muay Thai, Boxing, MMA

Scott is one of the original founders of Training Grounds and a black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, with a deep passion for Muay Thai, Kickboxing, Boxing, and MMA. A strong advocate for the ecological approach to coaching, he creates dynamic, game-based training centred on exploration, adaptability, and real-world application. Scott believes martial arts should be both practical and enjoyable, helping people thrive physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Head Boxing Coach

Jamie Rae

Boxing, Muay Thai, MMA, BJJ

Jamie is our Head Boxing Coach bringing years of experience in Muay Thai, MMA, and BJJ. Inspired by Bruce Lee movies and the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, his love for martial arts runs deep. Known for his calm energy and commitment to creating a welcoming space, Jamie is a big part of what makes the vibe at Training Grounds so special.

How We Coach

Ecological Approach

Where Applied

Learning emerges through interaction between the individual, the task, and the environment — helping students read situations, make decisions, and adapt in real time.

Constraint-Led Approach (CLA)

 

We shape learning by adjusting variables such as space, rules, and goals — encouraging creativity, awareness, and performance under pressure.

What This Means for Our Members

  • Engaging, game-based sessions that keep learning enjoyable
  • Better decision-making and awareness
  • Skills that transfer to live training and real situations
  • Increased confidence, adaptability, and long-term progress
