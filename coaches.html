Our Team

Meet the Coaches

Passionate martial artists with extensive experience, dedicated to helping you grow on and off the mats.

Head Coach & Founder

Scott

BJJ / MMA / Wrestling

Scott founded Training Grounds with a vision to create a martial arts community built on creativity, adaptability, and real growth. With deep expertise across BJJ, MMA, and Wrestling, Scott's coaching philosophy centres on problem-solving, movement, and building complete martial artists who think on their feet.

Head Boxing Coach

Jamie

Boxing

Jamie brings years of experience and a passion for the sweet science to Training Grounds. His coaching style blends Cuban, European, and modern boxing influences, focusing on footwork, rhythm, and helping each fighter find their own unique style. Sharp, technical, and always evolving.

BJJ & Wrestling Coach

Tristan

BJJ / Wrestling

Tristan is a dedicated grappler whose sessions are known for their intensity and creativity. Whether it's BJJ or Wrestling, Tristan focuses on live scenarios, real-time decision-making, and building the kind of instinctive movement that makes the difference on the mats.

Women's BJJ & Self Defence

Janise

Women's BJJ / Self Defence

Janise leads the Women's BJJ and Women's Self Defence programs with a focus on building confidence, practical skills, and a supportive training environment. She's passionate about empowering women through martial arts and creating a space where everyone feels welcome and capable.

Kids BJJ Coach

Wayne

Kids BJJ

Wayne is the heart of the TG Kids Academy. His approach to coaching young martial artists is all about fun, creativity, and building confidence through movement. Wayne's classes are packed with games, challenges, and age-appropriate skill development that keeps kids engaged and growing.

Boxing & Muay Thai Coach

Nathan

Boxing / Muay Thai

Nathan's striking coaching brings high energy and technical depth to every session. Whether it's Boxing or Muay Thai, Nathan focuses on building real striking skills through creative drills, live work, and a dynamic, movement-driven approach that keeps members sharp and progressing.

Boxing Coach

Jason

Boxing

Jason is a skilled boxing coach who brings solid fundamentals and sharp attention to detail. His sessions build strong technique through shadow work, pads, bags, and partner drills, always with a focus on practical skill development and finding the rhythm in the ring.
