Welcome to First Move

Our Women's Personal Safety Program

This isn't your typical self-defence class.

FIRST MOVE is built around real-life situations, helping you recognise what's happening early, make clear decisions, and move yourself to safety with confidence.

Using simple games and guided scenarios (think car parks, social settings, everyday moments), each session develops your instinct, awareness, and ability to respond, without pressure or overwhelm.

What You'll Learn

  • Sharpen your awareness and learn to trust your instincts
  • Set clear boundaries and use your voice with confidence
  • Move with purpose and find safe exits
  • Use simple, practical skills to break contact and get away
  • Apply everything through fun, guided scenarios

No tricky techniques to memorise.

No complicated choreography.

Just real skills you can actually use.
