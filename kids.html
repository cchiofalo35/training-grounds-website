Where Kids Learn Through Play

At TG Kids Martial Arts Academy, we offer BJJ (ages from 3 years) and Muay Thai (ages from 6 years) in a fun, playful setting that builds confidence, coordination, and self-defence.

Classes are movement-based and packed with games, challenges, and creativity, all led by experienced coaches who make learning exciting and community-focused.

Programs

Our Kids Programs

Ages 3-5

Little Grapplers BJJ

Our youngest martial artists learn through play-based BJJ sessions that develop balance, coordination, and body awareness. Classes are fun, safe, and designed to build confidence while introducing the basics of movement and grappling.

Ages 6-12

Kids BJJ

Our Kids BJJ program builds strong fundamentals through creative drills, games, and live rolling. Kids develop problem-solving skills, discipline, and resilience while learning practical self-defence in a supportive, team-oriented environment.

Ages 6+

Kids Muay Thai

Kids Muay Thai builds coordination, confidence, and striking technique through pad work, partner drills, and high-energy games. It's a great way for kids to stay active, learn a new skill, and build self-discipline in a fun group setting.
