Our Disciplines

Adults

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Adults

Muay Thai

Our Adults Muay Thai classes blend technique with a dynamic, movement-driven style. Creative, high-energy sessions that build real striking skills through live drills and fluid combos.

Boxing

Boxing at Training Grounds builds solid fundamentals, sharp movement, and encourages you to find your own unique style through shadow boxing, pads, bags, and partner work.

Adults

Wrestling

Wrestling at Training Grounds is dynamic and instinct-driven. We focus on live scenarios that build real-time decision-making, control, and explosive movement.

Adults

MMA

MMA training blends BJJ, Muay Thai, Boxing, and Wrestling into one dynamic system. You'll learn to move between styles, make decisions under pressure, and build real, adaptable skills.

Our Philosophy

The Training Grounds Way

At Training Grounds Martial Arts our approach to training focuses on adaptability, creativity, and problem-solving, helping members develop not only strength, agility, and endurance, but also sharper awareness, resilience, and confidence.

We create a space where people of all levels can grow at their own pace, supported by a strong, inclusive community. Whether you're stepping onto the mats for the first time or refining your skills in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, Boxing, or MMA, you'll be met with encouragement, challenge, and respect.

Our aim is to foster a healthy, collaborative environment where learning is dynamic, self-directed, and deeply engaging. Through martial arts, our members not only build physical and technical skill, but also develop self-belief, motivation, and a sense of belonging. We don't just train fighters, we grow capable, confident individuals who support each other and carry these lessons beyond the gym.
